# Concept B - Experience-first Studio

## Description
Lead with a high-impact visual redesign (Triad Canvas, Momentum Rings, and Heat Atlas), then gradually map current backend capabilities into the new UI before major domain changes.

## Pros
- Immediate perception shift from PoC to product.
- Strong differentiation in look and feel.
- Good for demos and stakeholder buy-in.

## Cons
- Risk of visual shell outrunning backend capability.
- Potential rework when domain model evolves.
- Higher chance of QA discovering behavior gaps.

## Estimated Effort
- Sprint 1: design system + major layout overhaul.
- Sprint 2+: backend/domain expansion catch-up.
